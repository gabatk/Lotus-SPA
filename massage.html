    • Types of massage offered in our SPA

    Hot stone massage

    Heated stones placed on the appropriate points of the body perfectly warm and relax tense muscles. The penetrating heat has a very soothing effect on the tissues of our body. Hot stone massage is very good for people who have overly tense muscles and prefer a gentler form of massage.

    Relaxation massage (aromatherapy)

    This type of massage is ideal for relieving muscle tension and will make us really relaxed. Relaxation massage using natural essential oils targets the appropriate needs of the person being massaged. The therapist can use various types of oils during this massage that cause relaxation, energize, reduce stress or restore balance to the body, etc.

    Shiatsu massage

    This uses finger pressure on the appropriate places (energy pathways) , which connect individual acupuncture points. Even after the first treatment, one can clearly feel a relaxed body, as well as mind. In addition, Shiatsu massage removes accumulated tension in our body, regulates metabolic processes and visibly improves health.

    Pregnancy massage

    This type of massage is becoming increasingly popular with women who are expecting the birth of a child. Pregnancy massage is used to reduce stress levels as well as swelling. It eases pains, reduces anxiety as well as depression. This type of massage is tailored to a woman's individual needs.
